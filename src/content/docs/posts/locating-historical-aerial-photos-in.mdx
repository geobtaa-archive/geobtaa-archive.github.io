The [BTAA   Geoportal](https://geo.btaa.org/) includes   historic aerial photography records that span institutions. This post focuses on searching and locating aerial   photography records in the BTAA Geoportal with a focus on Pennsylvania historical aerial photography collections.

 

 

***Overview   of historic aerial photos in the BTAA Geoportal***

 

A [search   for aerial photo](https://geo.btaa.org/?q=aerial+photo&amp;search_field=all_fields) yields   1,701 records (as   of October 2023) with a higher portion of   records seen from Pennsylvania, followed by Wisconsin, Minnesota, Ohio, Illinois, Nebraska, and New Jersey. The time period of   the aerial photo records located is   from the present back to the 1900-1949 time period,   with the most records present in the 1950 to 1999 time period. A   highlight of some resources will be provided here,   along with a greater focus on Pennsylvania historic aerial photo collections.

 

 
- [Wisconsin Historic Aerial Imagery Finder (Aerial Photos 1937-41)](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/5F3EEF4C-D1EA-4AC8-A2C5-774D21E78D46) from the University of Wisconsin-Madison provides an interactive application for users to identify and download images.
- Minnesota aerial photos from specific counties and timeframes are located, including multiple options for Dakota County, Minnesota, such as this [2018 imagery from the Minnesota Geospatial Commons.](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/b1df2f23-5914-4170-a4e6-cc4210231b86)
- Ohio aerial photos feature records part of the [Government Open Geospatial Data Collection](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/ba5cc745-21c5-4ae9-954b-72dd8db6815a), including the GEOhio Spatial Data Discovery Portal, such as these [2010 aerial photos of Dublin, Ohio.](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/dd5a6ff02a8747a4afbf2878742a7e91_0)
- Nebraska aerial photos are included from sources, such as the [Omaha/Douglas County GIS Open Data Portal](https://geo.btaa.org/?f%5Bschema_provider_s%5D%5B%5D=Omaha%2FDouglas+County+GIS+Open+Data+Portal) and the [Sarpy County GIS Portal](https://geo.btaa.org/?f%5Bschema_provider_s%5D%5B%5D=Sarpy+County+GIS+Portal).
- Illinois aerial photos includes multiple years available for Cook County, Illinois, including mosaic products, such as this one for [2017 from Cook Central](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/ee51815fb509461cb5f20714c7f0da98) through the Cook County Open Data site.
- New Jersey historical aerial photos include items from the Rutgers University Libraries, Special Collections and University Archives, such as this record for [Ocean County, New Jersey Lakewood aerial photos.](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/rutgers-lib:19721)
 

 

 

***Searching   for Pennsylvania historic aerial photos***

 

The   search for [aerial   photo and Pennsylvania](https://geo.btaa.org/?f%5Bdct_spatial_sm%5D%5B%5D=Pennsylvania&amp;q=aerial+photo&amp;search_field=all_fields) as   the place yielded 1,358 results (as of October 2023),   with a high number of records in the Montgomery County area that   come from the Montgomery County Pennsylvania Open Data site, such as this [1975   Montgomery County photo](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/07659c33ccfe4a4abd26cca3d15a0340_197).    While using other search strategies to use the term [aerial   photo and a bounding box for Pennsylvania](https://geo.btaa.org/?per_page=100&amp;search_field=all_fields&amp;q=aerial+photos&amp;bbox=-80.172729%2039.981329%20-76.25061%2041.808173) can yield   results that are available for the state, along with specific areas with the state. These records include results from the  [Pennsylvania   Spatial Data Access (PASDA)](https://www.pasda.psu.edu/) site related to historical aerial photos. A popular and commonly used resource is the [PennPilot   (Historical Aerial Photo Library) of photos from 1938 to 1980](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/pasda-1078),   which are also viewable in the [Pennsylvania   Imagery Navigator](http://maps.psiee.psu.edu/imagerynavigator/). In addition, some more specific collections include [Penn   State University Park/Centre County PA 1949 aerial photos](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/pasda-5082) and   [Penn State University Commonwealth and University Campuses 1985-1986 aerial photos](https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/pasda-5081) available   in PASDA and contributed by the [Donald   W. Hamer Center for Maps and Geospatial Information, Penn State University Libraries.](https://libraries.psu.edu/maps)

These   historical aerial photo collections can be useful to researchers and individuals seeking to   gain historical context to the area and to conduct additional research   on features of the landscape, such as buildings, forest cover, and/or other road features during their time   period.
