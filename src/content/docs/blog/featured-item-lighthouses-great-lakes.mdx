#### What is the item?

This data layer represents locations of Wisconsin lighthouses on the Great Lakes.<br/><br/>

#### What BTAA Library submitted the item?

University of Wisconsin<br/><br/>

#### Interesting tidbits:


- The month of August includes National Lighthouse Day on the 7th!
- This dataset is part of the Wisconsin Coastal Atlas, an online collection of geospatial data covering the coastal Wisconsin counties on Lakes Superior and Michigan.
- Each lighthouse point on the interactive map display is clickable for more information about that lighthouse, including name, location (latitude &amp; longitude), and links to outside websites for further reading.

#### Where can I find out more?


- To learn more about lighthouses all over the USA and Canada, see [Lighthousefriends.com](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.lighthousefriends.com%2Findex.html&amp;sa=D&amp;sntz=1&amp;usg=AFQjCNG7qRLqVPiYHq5oC--Mh6yeAGepkg). For lighthouses of Lakes Michigan, Superior, and Huron, see [Seeing the Light at terrypepper.com](http://www.google.com/url?q=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.terrypepper.com%2Flights%2Findex.htm&amp;sa=D&amp;sntz=1&amp;usg=AFQjCNFj-iQ7uU1mmE5UXOimzaE10wasKA).
- For more information on the scenic routes along the shorelines encircling Lakes Superior, Michigan, Huron, and Erie, see the [Great Lakes Circle Tour website](http://www.google.com/url?q=http%3A%2F%2Fgreatlakescircletour.org%2F&amp;sa=D&amp;sntz=1&amp;usg=AFQjCNH85gs7PY6Y4H4LWlfivVVEdd5ypw).
- To find out more about Great Lakes research, education, and outreach, see [University of Wisconsin Sea Grant](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.seagrant.wisc.edu%2F&amp;sa=D&amp;sntz=1&amp;usg=AFQjCNH4mDeqO_SgIz6FL5rUwQySTg5k_g).
