#### What is the item?

A shapefile with the Salt and Brine locations for Iowa Department of Transportation facilities.<br/><br/>

#### Interesting tidbits:


- Salt and Brine locations are where Iowa DOTâ€™s snow plows are kept.
- This data layer contains a polygon for each of these locations. Each polygon covers the entire area on which a facility sits. This enables two related geospatial applications: geofencing and automated vehicle location (AVL).
- In this case, the polygons act as geofences, meaning that as an AVL enabled snow plow enters or leaves the area of a polygon, the trip start or stop is recorded. That information can then be used for planning operations and communication to the public.
